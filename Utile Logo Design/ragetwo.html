The Rage Logo Two


The second logo uses a pixelated font and straight lines to emulate a raster display screen. The rounded corners of the background are inspired by the shape of the CRT monitor screens used in old arcade games and televisions. The three simple oblong shapes are inspired by PONG (1972), the game that started video game culture.

Brain Storming




The green colour is pure RGB green (0,255,0) and was chosen as it is the green found in early video games such as Space Invaders (1978) and Battlezone (1980).The font is a Google open font called Press Start 2P. and is a font inspired by the video game era. The glyph forms in this TrueType are based on those from "Return of Ishtar" (1986), one of the first games to include and regularly use lowercase as well as uppercase letters in its screen font.

Rage Iterations

The combination of straight lines and modern curves brings the imagery of the arcade era into the present and the logo translates well into black and white for use in print.
