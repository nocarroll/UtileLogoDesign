The Rage Logo One


This is the final logo. The logo emphasizes the use of the word rage showing it smashing into existence. The A in the logo is enlarged to make it seem as though it is trying to push its way through the logo and out into the forefront. Part of the inspiration behind this idea was the incredible hulk. He is small while human but then becomes enraged and larger than human once he is angry.

This logo is influenced by a comic book art aesthetic. The logo draws upon comic book imagery for example in the presentation of the letter “A” that resembles a superhero mask viewed sideways. The pattern across the bottom of the logo evokes imagery from Spider-Man, through its similarity to webs and also to the incredible hulk through it’s resemblance to an impacted building.

The initial part of the research for this concept focused upon the older style of comic art found in the 1960s and 1970s as we felt it would suit the aesthetic and brand of the R.A.G.E.

Brain Storming

Further research was conducted on examples of the comic book art style implemented as a design aesthetic in modern websites. The main sites that were researched for this were DC and Marvel comics. Whilst they do not make use of the purist comic style in their own sites, they implement a number of its elements in their designs.

Following this research development of the logo went through a number of different iterations before the group arrived on the version that was best suited to the client.

Letter Heads

Business Cards

Through analyzing the various iterations of the logo several aspects were identified as being successful and combined to create the final version. The color scheme was selected due to its simplicity as it translated successfully to a variety of forms of media such as print, web and advertising.
